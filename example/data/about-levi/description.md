_"I write code. I sing songs. I create things."_

Levi is a Software Engineer and maker. He sometimes makes exciting games. He sometimes makes complicated websites. He's constantly tinkering on side-projects&mdash;which are all highlighted in this portfolio.

Levi is from Washington state, where he currently lives with his partner and their daughter.

Outside the realm of computer science, Levi has many hobbies and interests. First and foremost, he is a musician. Levi is a classically trained singer and has led in many operas and musicals; some of his more notable performances include: [Dido and Aeneas][dido-and-aeneas] (Aeneas), [Savitri][savitri] (Satyavan), and [My Fair Lady][my-fair-lady] (Henry Higgins). Levi also [sings with a capella groups][love-like-you], [plays a lot of ukulele][down-today], and [composes chiptunes][compositions-playlist] for his side projects. Check out some of his [recordings on YouTube][youtube]!

Some of his other hobbies include: pottery, laser-cutting, brewing beer, gardening, baking, blacksmithing, tabletop games, and DIY home improvements.
